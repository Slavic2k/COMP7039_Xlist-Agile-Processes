Commodity prices for metals such as gold, silver, palladium, copper and nickel play an important role in global trade and economic stability. Precious metals (e.g gold, silver, platinum, palladium) are used as investment vehicles and certain valuable technologies, while industrial metals (e.g., copper, zinc, aluminum, nickel) are essential for manufacturing, infrastructure, and technology. Price movements in these commodities often reflect larger global events, ranging from financial crises to supply chain disruptions.
